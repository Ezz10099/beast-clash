## Purpose

This is the permanent terminology source for Arabic player-facing text, the Cell Runner, and fresh-player questions. Arabic must preserve the English meaning without adding coaching or extra explanation.

## Writing Rules

- Use concise, natural Modern Standard Arabic suitable for a mobile game.
- Keep the same term everywhere; do not replace **الجوهر** with **العنصر** or **القانون** with **طريقة الإطلاق**.
- Prefer direct verbs and short sentences over literal English word order.
- Preserve the information level of the English path. Do not explain a mechanic more clearly in Arabic than in English during a fresh-player gate.
- Translate dynamic HUD, canvas, accessibility, direct HTML, restart confirmation, and interview wording through the same glossary.
- Use minimal diacritics only when they prevent ambiguity.
- Owner phone feedback overrides a glossary phrase when it sounds unnatural; update this document and every dependent string together.
