# Beast Clash — Development Roadmap

## Roadmap Principles
- Build in playable increments.
- Validate fun and clarity before content expansion.
- Keep mobile portrait performance and readability as hard constraints.

## Phase 1 — Playable Vertical Slice

**Goal:** one complete, fun combat loop.

**Scope**
- 3v3 auto-battle in portrait.
- 6 MVP animals (Gorilla, Tiger, Crocodile, Eagle, Viper, Rhino).
- Basic attacks + one ultimate + one passive per animal.
- Match flow: start, combat, result screen, replay.
- Minimal UI for team preview and match outcome.

**Exit criteria**
- Battles are understandable on first watch.
- Average match duration near 60–120s.
- No major readability blockers in effects/UI.

## Phase 2 — Team Selection and Progression

**Goal:** add player agency and light retention.

**Scope**
- Team selection screen with role indicators.
- Starter progression (animal level bands or simple upgrades).
- Reward loop for wins/losses.
- Basic balance pass for role viability.

**Exit criteria**
- Players can create at least 2 viable team archetypes.
- Progression feels meaningful without grind walls.

## Phase 3 — Animal Collection

**Goal:** expand roster motivation safely.

**Scope**
- Collection UX (owned/unowned, role/tier filters).
- Controlled roster growth (small batch releases).
- Tier-based unlock paths (Common -> Apex entry).

**Exit criteria**
- Collection system is clear and stable.
- New animals do not reduce combat readability.

## Phase 4 — Campaign and Biomes

**Goal:** deepen long-term structure.

**Scope**
- Biome-based chapters/arenas.
- Biome affinity bonuses.
- Intro encounters for rare biome animals and early bosses.

**Exit criteria**
- Biomes change strategy without overwhelming complexity.
- Difficulty curve supports both new and returning players.

## Phase 5 — Art, Sound, and Polish

**Goal:** finalize feel, identity, and quality.

**Scope**
- Dark fantasy arena art pass.
- Audio pass (hit impact, creature cues, UI feedback).
- Performance optimization for target mobile devices.
- UX polish, onboarding clarity, accessibility checks.

**Exit criteria**
- Stable FPS and input responsiveness on target phones.
- Visual/audio identity feels cohesive and premium.

## Do Not Overbuild (Applied to Every Phase)

- Keep MVP small and testable.
- Do not rush to 100 animals.
- Polish first 6 animals before major roster expansion.
- Prefer improving existing systems over adding parallel systems.
